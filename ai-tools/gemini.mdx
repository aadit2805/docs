Google's Gemini models are a family of multimodal AI models built for reasoning, coding, and long-context workloads. This page summarizes the current lineup and helps you pick the right model for your use case.

## Model family

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Gemini 2.5 Pro" icon="brain">
    Flagship model for complex reasoning, advanced coding, and multimodal tasks that need the highest quality.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gemini 2.5 Flash" icon="bolt">
    Fast, cost-efficient model tuned for high-throughput production workloads with strong quality.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gemini 2.5 Flash-Lite" icon="feather">
    The lightest, lowest-latency option. A good fit for classification, extraction, and simple assistants.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gemini Embedding" icon="vector-square">
    Embedding model for search, retrieval, and RAG over your documentation.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Choosing a model

- **Reach for Gemini 2.5 Pro** when you need top-tier quality across text, code, images, and long context.
- **Reach for Gemini 2.5 Flash** for day-to-day production work where latency and cost matter alongside quality.
- **Reach for Gemini 2.5 Flash-Lite** for high-volume, simple tasks where speed and price dominate.
- **Reach for Gemini Embedding** when you're building semantic search or RAG on top of your docs.

## Using Gemini with your docs

You can wire Gemini into your documentation workflow:

- Use the Gemini API to draft, summarize, or translate pages, and to generate examples and FAQs from existing content.
- Feed full pages or entire sections into Gemini's long context window for site-wide edits and consistency passes.
- Pair Gemini Embedding with your docs to power a custom search or retrieval experience.
